My Education

Stevens Institute of Technology

My favorite class so far has been Web Programming I (no bs, I've learned sooo much and can't wait to continue learning and adding skills to my toolbox!!!).

My favorite memory so far has been winning second place in 'Ducks on Display' and performing at the Audio Engineering Club's Open Mic Night my second year at Stevens. When I started college, I put my hobbies on hold so I could focus on acclimating to college, especially since I am a commuter. So, my second year at Stevens, I decided to pursue my passions again and signed up for the talent show. I had soooo much fun! I also performed at AEC's open mic night with some talented musician friends, we did 'Faithfully' by Journey (they played, I sang) for the Valentine's themed Open Mic night. Boy, those two nights were an absolute BLAST!

Passaic Valley Regional High School

My favorite class in high school was Robotics. Because of that class, I discovered how much I truly loved tech, and made the decision right then and there to pursue engineering! I don't know what I would have done or what school I would have gone to, if it weren't for that class.

My favorite memory in high school? Oh boy where do I even start! I can't pick just one, so I'll go ahead and say theatre. Any time I was apart of a show, was a time I was genuinely so so happy and fulfilled. We put on some of the best shows and went to a lot of competitions, I miss it!
