Me, Myself, and I: Eleni Rotsides

A Bio About Lil 'ole Me:

Hello to whomever may be reading! My name is Eleni (rhymes with 'a penny') Rotsides and I am a rising 4/5 undergraduate Software Engineering major. Aside from my major, I enjoy singing and teaching myself new instruments. I've been singing ever since I could remember, and even won 2nd place in 'Ducks on Display', the Stevens talent show, last year by performing a comedic musical theatre piece called 'Taylor the Latte Boy'! I am also a member of the Stevens Choir and the President's Ensemble at Stevens! In highschool, I did lots and lots of theatre, too. I am a hugeeee metalhead and music lover, and if I'm being honest, I could listen to anything really (some of my choices in music may seem questionable to some lol). Right now, I'm enjoying learning my favorite riffs on the electric guitar, and my plan is to start diving more into theory when school dies down a bit! My goal? I'd LOVE to be able to play a Tool song in its entierty, but their music is very complex in nature so it may take me a little while to be able to do this. I'll call that one a long term goal, for sure. Music teaches me all about patience and discipline; two traits that I believe are crucial to posses in the work force, especially as a software engineer. I've always loved math, science, and computers, but reallyyyy discovered the depth of my love of technology when I took a Robotics class (my highschool's only tech class) in the 10th grade. There, I realized I enjoyed the computer aspect of engineering and never looked back!
